Riverbend Hawks Football Team Undefeated in Pre-Season

Published: August 25, 2025 | By: Sarah Rodriguez

The Riverbend High School Hawks football team continues their winning streak with a decisive 28-14 victory over the Central City Eagles last Friday night. This marks their fourth consecutive pre-season win.

Head coach Mark Stevens praised the team's dedication: "These young men have been working incredibly hard all summer. Their commitment to excellence is showing on the field."

Elementary School Science Fair Winners Announced

Published: August 23, 2025 | By: Lisa Chen

Riverbend Elementary School held its annual science fair, with over 150 student projects on display. The winning projects will advance to the regional competition next month.

Winners:

  • First Place: Emma Thompson - "Solar Power for Clean Water"
  • Second Place: Marcus Williams - "Plant Growth in Different Soils"
  • Third Place: Ava Johnson - "The Science of Recycling"
